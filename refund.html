Refund Policy

This policy explains when account funding, shop activity, and administrative corrections may qualify for review or refund.

1. Account funding

Wallet funding and Stax credits are generally treated as final once the payment has been successfully processed, except where required by law or where a clear platform, duplicate-charge, or technical error has occurred.

2. Goal commitments

Committed Stax used for a goal is subject to the goal rules accepted at launch. Failed or missed goals are not automatically refundable. Administrative review may correct clear platform errors or verified account issues.

3. Shop orders

Orders may be canceled by the platform when fulfillment is unavailable, shipping information is incomplete, or fraud is suspected. When an order is canceled by the platform, spent Stax may be returned to the user balance.

4. How to request review

To request review of a payment, balance issue, or order problem, contact support with your account email, date of the issue, and a description of what happened.
