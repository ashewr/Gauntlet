Privacy Policy

This policy explains what information The Gauntlet collects, how it is used, and how users can contact support with privacy questions.

1. Information we collect

  • Account information such as name, email address, username, and password hash.
  • Goal, proof, order, wallet, and account activity records required to operate the service.
  • Technical information such as session activity, device access timing, and basic request logs.

2. How information is used

Information is used to authenticate accounts, track goals, review proof, operate the wallet and store experience, prevent abuse, investigate suspicious activity, and respond to support requests.

3. Sharing

The Gauntlet may share limited information with service providers that help operate payments, hosting, storage, analytics, fraud prevention, and customer support. Information may also be disclosed when required by law or to protect the platform and its users.

4. Security

Reasonable technical and administrative safeguards are used to protect account and transaction data. No system is completely risk free, so users should also protect their credentials and device access.

5. Data retention

Account, proof, payment, and support records may be retained as long as needed for business operations, legal obligations, fraud prevention, and dispute resolution.

6. Contact

Privacy questions can be sent to joingauntlet@gmail.com.
